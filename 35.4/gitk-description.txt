The upper left window gives information concerning the structure of the
repository.  It shows where the master, head, and branches are and also
shows commits.
The top middle window gives information about the name and email of users.
The window to the right of that shows the date and time of the commit.
The left middle window shows information about the SHA1 ID, and a search
bar that can search for commites containing input.
The bottom left window shows the ChangeLog file which contains information
about the commit, the author, the email, the time, the parent and child, as
well as brances and follows.
The bottom right window shows a list of files that were impacted by the
commit that we selected.
